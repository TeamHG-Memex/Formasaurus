Why ClientTrack?

Integrated health systems enable health and human service organizations to embrace a holistic care model where better information drives better outcomes. As organizations recognize the importance of understanding the social determinants of health and work to facilitate care coordination across healthcare and social service providers, better information emerges which can result in better patient outcomes. Rather than providing data only focused on episodic care, ClientTrack's advanced case management connects the dots across the care continuum and allows a much clearer picture to emerge.
