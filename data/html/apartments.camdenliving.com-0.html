Adams Morgan, Dupont Circle, the U Street Corridor and other Washington D.C. hot spots are just steps from your door when you live at Camden Roosevelt. As one of our residents, you will be overwhelmed by the exquisite attention to detail blended with modern amenities in each of our studio, one and two bedroom apartment homes. The renovated historic residences include features like hardwood floors, granite countertops, crown molding and include washers and dryers. Enjoy Wi-Fi access in the resident lounge, work out in our state-of-the-art fitness center or store your favorite vintage in the wine cellar. Other features include a 24-hour concierge service and valet parking garage as well as dry cleaning service. Camden Roosevelt is ideally located in Washington D.C. and the Metro is only blocks away while a Zip Car is right at your doorstep.
